Winning Tips & Strategy

Clock Management

Save timeouts for in‑bounds tackles and tricky situations. Use quick outs to stop the clock and keep momentum.

Down & Distance

On 3rd and long, choose routes that allow YAC (yards after catch). On 2nd and short, consider a deeper shot.

Play Calling

Mix quick slants, outs, and an occasional draw. Once you cross midfield, keep field‑goal range in mind.

Protect the Ball

Interceptions and sacks are clock killers. Throw the ball away if nothing’s open and the pocket collapses.
