Winning Tips for 2 Minute Football

Clock Management

Save timeouts for situations where the clock would otherwise keep running (e.g., in‑bounds tackles). Get out of bounds on short routes to stop the clock.

Play Calling

Use quick outs and slants to move the chains, then take a calculated deep shot to flip the field. Mix in draws to punish aggressive coverage.

Field Position

Past midfield, think about realistic field‑goal range and the down‑and‑distance needed to keep your drive alive.

Situational Awareness

  • On 3rd and long, prioritize catch‑and‑run routes (maximize YAC).
  • Use the two‑minute warning as a free timeout when applicable.
